How It Works

Our marketplaces offer a one-stop location for all sales and transfers. We offer fans the ability to buy and sell seat locations online (U.S. Patent 7099841), as well as the ability to complete Private Transfers (transfers to family or friends, as well as transfers due to death or divorce).Â 

Each marketplace offers unique tools to buyers and sellers that improve the user experience. Some of these tools are: message center, watch list, wish list, 3D seating charts, real views from sections, sales data features and e-signatures.

How to Sell

Create a listing with your account and seat information

Accept or Reject offers within 48 hours before they expire, you can also counter offer.

Agree on which Tickets are included in the transaction.

Buyer submits payments after ticket terms have been agreed to.

Fill and Sign Forms for the transfer of ownership.

Team ticket office approves, funds and tickets are released to each party.
